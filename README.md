CivicDNA – AI-Powered Urban Digital Twin & Governance Intelligence Platform
Overview

CivicDNA is a next-generation AI Governance Platform designed to help governments, municipal corporations, smart city authorities, disaster management agencies, and policy makers make proactive, data-driven decisions.

The platform creates a Digital Civic DNA of a city by continuously analyzing traffic, pollution, healthcare, crime, weather, emergency response, energy consumption, water supply, population density, and citizen grievance data.

Using Artificial Intelligence, Predictive Analytics, Urban Simulations, and a 3D Digital City Twin, CivicDNA enables authorities to predict risks, evaluate government schemes, optimize resource allocation, and improve urban resilience.
